Mechanistic Elucidation & Stereochemical Control in AMDase

Our work on Arylmalonate Decarboxylase (AMDase) focuses on understanding the alternative substrate binding modes and how they affect product stereochemistry. Using enzyme engineering, QM/MM modeling, and enhanced sampling, we dissected the mechanistic origins of catalytic preference and leveraged this insight to improve selectivity in non-native reactions.

Highlights

  • Elucidated alternative binding modes of alkenyl substrates in AMDase.
  • Integrated computation and experiment to explain stereochemical outcomes.
  • Published in Journal of the American Chemical Society.
